10. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do you charge a fixed retainer instead of a percentage of ad spend?

Percentage-based pricing creates a conflict of interest — agencies earn more when you spend more, regardless of performance. Our fixed retainer aligns our incentives with yours: we succeed when you get results, not when budgets increase. This ensures we optimize for efficiency, not just spend.

Q: What if the recommended ad budget doesn't work for our cash flow?

The ₹1,50,000/month ad budget is a recommendation based on competitive benchmarks and expected ROI. We can start with a lower budget (minimum ₹80,000/month) and scale up as we prove performance. However, medical tourism is competitive — lower budgets mean fewer clicks, longer time to results.

Q: Can we pause the engagement after 3 months if results aren't meeting expectations?

While this is structured as a 6-month engagement, we include a performance checkpoint at Month 3. If results are significantly below agreed KPIs and we cannot identify a clear path to improvement, we can mutually agree to pause or adjust the scope. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into underperforming contracts.

Q: How do you measure success? What are the key KPIs?

Primary KPIs: (1) Qualified leads per month (from all channels), (2) Cost per lead (target: under ₹3,500 by Month 6), (3) Lead-to-consultation conversion rate, (4) Organic traffic growth, (5) Keyword rankings in top 10. Monthly reports include full-funnel metrics from impressions to patient bookings (when data is available).

Q: What happens to all the content, ads, and assets you create?

You own 100% of everything we create — landing pages, blog posts, ad creatives, email sequences, automation workflows, social media content. If the engagement ends, you retain full access and ownership. We don't hold your assets hostage.

Q: Do you work with our competitors?

We do not work with direct competitors in the same geographic market simultaneously. For the duration of this engagement, we will not take on other medical tourism facilitators or virtual hospital platforms targeting India + international patients without your prior consent.

Q: What if we already have an in-house marketing team?

We work collaboratively with in-house teams. Think of us as an extension of your marketing department — we bring specialized SEO, paid ads, and automation expertise. Your team retains strategic control, brand oversight, and final approvals. We execute, optimize, and report.

Q: How quickly will we see results?

Paid ads deliver immediate traffic (Week 1-2). Social media engagement builds over 4-6 weeks. SEO takes 8-12 weeks to show meaningful ranking improvements (Google's algorithm update cycles). Full ROI visibility typically emerges by Month 4-5. We provide weekly dashboards so you see progress continuously, not just at the end.

Q: What if our website needs major technical changes or redesign?

We include essential technical SEO fixes (Core Web Vitals, schema markup, heading structure, accessibility). If a full redesign or platform migration is needed, we can scope that separately. However, most SEO and CRO improvements can be achieved through incremental optimizations without a complete rebuild.

Q: Can you help us get patient testimonials and case studies?

Yes — as part of content strategy, we can design patient interview questionnaires, video testimonial scripts, and case study templates. However, patient outreach, consent collection, and HIPAA/data privacy compliance remain MedicoExperts' responsibility. We shape the stories; you source the participants.
